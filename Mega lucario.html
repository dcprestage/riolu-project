Mega Lucario

Who/What is Mega Lucario

Mega Lucario is, as the name sounds, an evolution or stronger form of Lucario that was introduced in generation 6 with the introduction of Mega Evolution which is something that allows certain Pokemon to evolve a stage past their final evolution. The Black streaks all over its body show where its aura and the mega energy intermingled and raced through allowing it to produce stronger aura. But with this boosted stength and power comes some downsides with it. The huge boost in power is said to heighten its combat instincts so much so that it makes the Lucario governed almost by combat instincts solely, making its fighting style almost merciless and heartless

Abilities and Stats

Unlike Riolu and regular Lucario, Mega Lucario only has 1 ability:

  1. Adaptability

Mega Lucario has a base stat total of 625:

  • HP: 70
  • Attack: 145
  • Defense: 88
  • Sp. Attack: 140
  • Sp. Defense: 70
  • Speed: 112

Mega Lucario has an EV yield of 1 Attack EV and 1 Special Attack EV and a base friendship value of 50. Its base catch rate (with a pokeball and full HP) is 45 or 5.9%.
