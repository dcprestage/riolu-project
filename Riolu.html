Riolu

Who/What is Riolu

Riolu is a fighting type pokemon that was introduced in generation 4 and is a fan favorite among the pokemon community. Known as the 'Emanation Pokemon', Riolu is said to use waves called auras to determine how a person or pokemon feels and is also able to use these auras to communicate with other Riolu. It is also known to be very energetic with enough stamina to run through the night.

Abilities and Stats

 

Riolu has 2 main abilities and 1 hidden ability:

  1. Steadfast
  2. Inner Focus
  3. Prankster (hidden ability)

Riolu has a base stat total of 285:

  • HP: 40
  • Attack: 70
  • Defense: 40
  • Sp. Attack: 35
  • Sp. Defense: 40
  • Speed: 60

Has an EV yield of 1 Attack EV and a base friendship value of 50. Its base catch rate (with a pokeball and full HP) is 75 or 9.8%
