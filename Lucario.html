Lucario

Who/What is Lucario

Lucario is a Fighting and Steel type pokemon that was indroduced in generation 4 as the evolution of Riolu and is another popular fan favorite among the pokemon community. Riolu evolves into lucario when it is leveled up during the day while having high friendship. Lucario is known as the 'Aura Pokemon', and similar to riolu lucario uses aura but stronger and more controlled, lucario can use auras to sense and locate pokemon and human and can use the auras to detect their emotions from over half a mile away. It can control and manipulate auras to be used while in combat fighting. Lucario is even said to be able to use auras to read others thoughts and movements

Abilities and Stats

Same as Riolu, Lucario also has 2 main abilities and 1 hidden ability:

  1. Steadfast
  2. Inner Focus
  3. Justified (hidden ability)

Lucario has a base stat total of 525:

  • HP: 70
  • Attack: 110
  • Defense: 70
  • Sp. Attack: 115
  • Sp. Defense: 70
  • Speed: 90

Lucario has an EV yield of 1 Attack EV and 1 Special Attack EV and a base friendship value of 50. Its base catch rate (with a pokeball and full HP) is 45 or 5.9%.
